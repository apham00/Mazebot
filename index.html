What is Moorebot Scout?

The Moorebot Scout is a compact, autonomous mobile robot platform used in our robotics research project. Our team is programming it to navigate environments, avoid obstacles, and complete tasks — all without human control.

Project Overview

This project is part of our coursework in the Department of Computer Science at Lamar University. We are exploring topics in autonomous navigation, sensor fusion, and real-time control systems using the Moorebot Scout hardware platform.

Goals & Objectives

Our primary objectives include implementing obstacle detection and avoidance, mapping a known environment, and executing a predefined navigation route autonomously. We are also experimenting with camera-based object recognition as a stretch goal.
